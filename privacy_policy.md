# Privacy Policy

**Better Fab**

Better Fab is a browser extension designed to enhance the Fab.com browsing experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

### Data Collection and Usage
- **No Personal Data Collected:** Better Fab does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.
- **Local Storage Only:** Any settings, configurations, or blocklists (e.g., hidden sellers or saved filters) are saved locally on your device within your browser's standard local storage. 
- **No Analytics or Tracking:** This extension does not use any third-party analytics software, tracking scripts, or telemetry to monitor your usage.

### Extension Permissions
- **Storage:** Required solely to save your local preferences and filter configurations.
- **Downloads:** Required solely to allow the extension to automatically download free items to your library when the auto-claim feature is used.
- **Host Permissions (`*://*.fab.com/*`):** Required to run the extension's scripts on Fab.com in order to manipulate the page layout, add seller metrics, and apply your local filters.

### Changes to this Policy
If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this document. However, our core commitment to keeping your data local and secure will not change.

### Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please visit our repository: [https://codeberg.org/renews/better-fab](https://codeberg.org/renews/better-fab).
